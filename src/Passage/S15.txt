1. 答案：D

解析：问题中问的是戏剧评论家的看法，定位至原文“The New Stagecraft rejected theatrical literalism; it drew inspiration from the subjectivity and minimalism of modern painters, advocating simplified sets designed to express a dramatic texts central ideas. ……a trueartist eliminated the inessential to create more meaningful, expressive stage images. ”可知选B选项。
